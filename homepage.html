Welcome

INDIAN CUISINE

The Concept

✻

Our passion is a contemporary restaurant offering dining experiences to satisfy all aspects of fine cuisine: wine selection, service, ambience and atmosphere. Our menu reflects the diversity of India, steeped in the classics while offering touches of modernity. Our service style emphasizes hospitality with knowledgeable, technically proficient staff who are present yet unobtrusive. The result is a vibrant and trendsetting scene, full of energy and sophistication. Le Jardin is a place of gathering, a place of celebration, a place for business and a place for pleasure.

DECOR

Refined Dining

✻

Old World Indian artistry and modern elegance with natural elements throughout the space, hand-chiseled ribbons of carved black Indian limestone, sand stone statues, antique wood carvings and Venetian like mirrors set the tones for a unique NYC dining experience.

OUR NECTARS

Wine Selection

✻

Le Jardin’s wine program recently awarded a coveted “Best of Award of Excellence” by Wine Spectator and “Three Stars” by World of Fine Wine is another leading pillar of Le Jardin that separates its modern concept from the pack.
